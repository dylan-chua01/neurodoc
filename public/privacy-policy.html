Your privacy is our top priority

Effective Date: June 15, 2025
At NeuroLearn, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

Such as your name, email address, and account credentials when you sign up.

Learning Data

Including your session history, quiz results, progress tracking, and preferences.

Uploaded Content

Such as PDFs or documents you upload for personalized learning.

Usage Data

Including browser type, device type, IP address, and interaction logs.

Cookies and Analytics

To improve performance and personalize your experience.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track progress and generate insights
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Communicate updates, features, and promotions
  • Ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations

3 Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal data

Your privacy and trust are fundamental to our mission

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: trusted third parties who help us operate our platform (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Authorities: if required by law or in response to a legal request
  • Affiliates or Partners: only with your consent

4 Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and safeguard your credentials.

5 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@neurolearn.ai.

6 Children's Privacy

NeuroLearn is designed to be accessible and beneficial to learners of all ages. However, we recommend that children under the age of 13 use the platform under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent. If we learn that we have unintentionally collected such information, we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
